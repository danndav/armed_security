MORE THAN JUST SECURITY... IT'S ARMED INTELLIGENT SECURITY

Our Armed Security Officers protect private and public property and ensure the safety of staff, customers, personnel and the public.  Our Armed Security Guards can work to provide security protection and support for private companies, armored car services, retail shops or other security firms. Our Armed Security Officers are licensed to carry weapons and firearms, while carrying out their duties of protecting an individual or property.

Find Out More

When you hire our Armed Security Officers you are assured of:

• Recording and Reporting Duties: Our Armed Security Officers play an important role when it comes to retrieving information needed for, say, evidence to a crime. It is they who record the particular happenings in their stations and periodically report to their superior. Reporting of activities also involves alarming the whole security team in a location in case there is violation of a rule within their premises. The officers also apprehend suspects and retain them until a time when other government forces can take over the situation.

• Operating Equipment Our Armed Security Officers play an important role in operating security equipment within their stations. Video cameras, security alarms, body scanners and fire equipment are some examples of equipment they operate. Periodical testing of these pieces of equipment also qualifies under this role. Operating equipment also includes taking charge of a situation such as directing or assembling people in case of a fire, bomb threat or flooding within their stationed workplace.

• Transporting Valuables Our Armed Security Officers work for Banks, Insurance Firms, Local Government Offices, Precious Metal Mining Companies and even Hyper Malls, when they need to transport their valuables such as cash and gold to safer destinations. Our Armed Security Officers performing such tasks usually have additional armory such as bulletproof vests and powerful firearms. Additionally the types of vehicles they operate usually have additional security enforcements. For instance, the transporting vans may be heavily hammered and their glassware shielded using sieve wires for extra protection.
