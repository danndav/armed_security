CYBER SECURITY



We provide Cyber Security or Information Technology or Information Technology Security or Electronic Information Security to protect computers, networks, programs and data from unauthorized access or attacks that are aimed for exploitation.
We use Cyber Security for defending computers, servers, mobile devices, electronic systems, networks, and data from malicious attacks. We will use state of the art technology to protect your computer systems and their components – including hardware, software and data – and digital infrastructure from attack, unauthorized access or being otherwise damaged or made inaccessible.

When you subscribe to our Cyber Security Services you are assured of:

• unique, effective security strategies for software systems, networks, data centers, and hardware

• implementation of security systems into your software, hardware, and components

• researching best ways to secure your company-wide IT infrastructure

• building firewalls to protect your network infrastructures

• quality assurance of your software and hardware for security vulnerabilities and risks

• closing off any security vulnerability in the case of an attack

• identifying cyber attackers, reporting to upper management, and cooperating with police or other legal forces to detain perpetrator

• working independently or as part of a team as needed

VEHICLE PATROL

We provide Patrol Vehicles for monitoring our security stations and responding to emergencies in good time. Our Patrol Vehicles are fitted with two-way radio and computer and connected with our headquarters for centralized security management. We use our Patrol Vehicles for transporting Security Officers so they can reach the scene of an incident quickly or to patrol an area, all while providing a visible deterrent to crime. Our Patrol Vehicles are able to respond to emergencies, and as such are fitted with visual and audible warnings.




GPS TRACKING



We provide Global Positioning System tracking service which is a method of working out exactly where something is. We can place a GPS tracking system, in a vehicle, on a cell phone, or on special GPS devices, which can either be a fixed or portable unit. Our GPS works by providing information on exact location. It can also track the movement of a vehicle or person. Our GPS Tracking System has a wide range of applications and can be used to track a car, a pet, an old man or a child. Our personal GPS Tracker can transmit positioning information to your Smartphone or computer.
